## Tests

### Compliance

Compliance tests are used to varify if a sample or a stat is compatible with a theorical/hypotesis distriburtion.

### Goodness of fit

Compare data with a priori chosen distribution

### Homogeneity

Verify whether K samples (groups) come from the same population (the distribution of the variable of interest is the same across the K samples)

### Independance (association)

Independence tests are used to determine whether a relationship exists between two variables. The techniques used differ depending on whether the variables.(nominal, qualitative, ordinal, or quantitative)
